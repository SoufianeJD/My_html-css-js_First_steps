The impossible sherlock holmes escape room !

- Take down the bomb -

With two locations in The Netherlands, Sherlocked boasts two cinematic experiences in the basement of their century-old Amsterdam location. The first, The Architect, is a mystery escape room with a standard 60-minute time limit. However, their other room comes with a lot more intrigue. The Vault provides players with 90 minutes and turns typical escape rooms on their heads as players take on the role of thieves attempting to steal a mysterious object from a 90-year-old safe. The game even makes use of an actor and is less focused on puzzles, and more so on the adventure. The Vault is touted as one of the most challenging escape rooms, winning the Golden Lock-In Award as one the best escape rooms in the world.
